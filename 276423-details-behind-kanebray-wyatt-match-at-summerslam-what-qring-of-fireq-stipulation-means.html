Details Behind Kane/Bray Wyatt Match at SummerSlam, What Ring of Fire Stipulation Means

March 14, 2024
blog

The "Ring of Fire" match at SummerSlam between Kane and Bray Wyatt will essentially be an Inferno Match, reports PWInsider.

Fire will surround the ring to keep anyone from getting involved with the match. However, the difference will be that the winner isn't determined by setting your opponent on fire. 

As we previously reported, this match could be a way to write Kane off television as he's set to film the sequel to See No Evil this fall.
