➡ Jonita Gandhi Biography

Jonita Gandhi an Indian playback singer who sings for Hindi and Tamil language films. She has been in this field since a long and has been enthralling a larger audience. Apart from singing, she has her online presence on the topmost streaming channel YouTube. In 2013, the star took the start of her singing career through a Bollywood film named ‘Chennai Express’ for which she sang a title song. Since then, she has been giving many popular hits and music lovers enjoy her melodious voice and lovely songs. Well, there are many achievements that she got during her career life and much acclaim from the audience but many of us don’t know much about her personal and professional life. So, here we are bringing you some lesser facts to know more about the gorgeous lady. Let us have a look!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Jonita Gandhi

1- Realname and Nickname

Jonita Gandhi was born on 23rd October 1989 in Delhi, India where she raised and grew up. She was born as Jonita Gandhi but later, she became popular by her nickname which is Toronto’s Nightingale. Wow…So impressive!

2- Beauty with Brains

We can call her beauty with brains as Jonita is not just a great, successful singer, yet she is a well-educated lady who pursued a bachelors degree in the field of Health Sciences and did honors in the field of Business Administration.

3- She & Deepak Gandhi

Do you know Deepak Gandhi? Let us tell you that Jonita Gandhi is the daughter of Deepak Gandhi who is a famed Electronics Engineer who runs a Music Studio. That’s inspiring to know about her!

4- Hobbies

During her spare time, the singer, Jonita Gandhi loves spending her time traveling and shopping. Once she revealed that she loves enjoying shopping and buying her favorite matching accessories.

5- Favorite Food

Being a foodie, her favorite food is Tofu, Sushi, Aamras Puri, and Dosa. And all these dishes are Indian traditional dishes and everyone loves to taste such kind of food items. It seems she is a spice lover!

6- Favorite Artists

Her most favorite Bollywood actress is Alia Bhatt who is known for her brilliant acting chops. While among singers, her most favorite singers are A.R.Rahman, Lata Mangeshkar, Asha Bhosle, and R.D.Burman.

7- Favorite TV Shows/Music Genres

Her most favorite television shows are Dexter, Silicon Valley, The Big Bang Theory, and Girls. While her favorite music genres are romance, classical music, and opera.

8- Internee at CIBC

During her graduation, Jonita Gandhi did an internship at CIBC World Markets. Once she revealed that she aspired to become a Corporate Banker, but later, she developed her interest in the field of music.
