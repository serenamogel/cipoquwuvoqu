Know About Alisyn Camerota; Age, Fox News, CNN, Salary, Husband

In the mass of media personalities, there are many veteran reporters who are still acquiring immense success from the visionary platform. Among them, CNN’s morning show host Alisyn Camerota is the inspiring name.

Alisyn Camerota has devoted a significant part of her age in media and journalism. While doing so, she has traveled to major broadcasting channels including Fox News and CNN. In the middle of that, Alisyn always strikes out as a perfect family woman biding her time for her husband and three beautiful kids.

Short Bio: Age, Ethnicity, Height

Alisyn Camerota came to this world as Alisyn Lane Camerota on 21 June 1966. The 52-years-of-age media star hails from her hometown of Bellingham, Washington. Alisyn, who holds mixed (Italian and American) ethnicity and American nationality, grew up alongside her brother who currently serves as an abdominal surgeon.

Standing at a decent height of 5 feet and 4 inches (1.64 m), Alisyn graduated with a degree in broadcast journalism from the School of Communication of the American University in Washington, D.C. Immediately, she pitched her career in broadcasting.

Career Highlights: Fox News & CNN

Since joining the FOX News as a correspondent for the network’s Boston bureau in February 1998, Alisyn devoted her time reporting a wide variety of stories and contributing to its affiliate service- Fox News Edge. Later in October 2007, she initiated a self-created blog named In The Greenroom on the Fox & Friends page on Fox News Channel’s website

Besides, Alisyn co-hosted America’s News Headquarters since 30 September 2013 before announcing the end of her 16-year stint at the network on 14 March 2014.

Alisyn Camerota alongside her CNN colleague, John Berman on 18 January 2019 (Picture: Alisyn’s Instagram)

The media persona kicked off her CNN career joining the news team on 14 July 2014. She became the permanent co-anchor of the New Day in 2015 after previously co-anchoring the show on 25 July 2014 and 25 August 2015.

Salary & Net Worth

Given her illustrious career in the media field, the veteran journalist amasses a hefty net worth of around $7.5 Million. Besides, Alisyn’s base salary as the CNN reporter falls between $67K to $95K with total pay somewhat surfacing under $70K to $99K.

Who Is Alisyn Camerota’s Husband?

Alisyn Camerota is soaking all the bliss from her relationship life thanks to her caring husband cum friend, Tim Lewis. Tim is an investor who has given two decades of his career in leadership positions. He currently serves as a partner at Southfield Capital in Greenwich, Connecticut.

Alisyn Camerota pictured with her husband, Tim Lewis on 9 August 2018 (Picture: Alisyn’s Instagram)

Alisyn and Tim tied the wedding knot in 2002. After that, the couple tried to conceive a child for a while but failed miserably with two pregnancies resulting in miscarriages. Alisyn publicly spoke about her battle with fertility issues and how her husband supported her in that perverse situation.

Fortunately, Alisyn and her fiancé got the opportunity to enjoy parenthood as they welcomed fraternal twin daughters: Francesca and Alessandra in 2005. Two years later, the pair was blessed with a son named Nathaniel.

Currently, Alisyn is enjoying the family time residing in her $3 Million-worth home in Westport, Connecticut.

Some Interesting Facts About Alisyn Camerota

  • Before starting her career with Fox News, Alisyn served for WHDH and WTTG.
  • In her media career, she has covered the aftermath of Hurricane Harvey in Houston as well as the Paris and Brussels terror attacks.
  • She has received two Emmy nominations for news reporting during her career.
  • Alisyn only realized that she was pregnant with her son after 16 weeks.
  • Alisyn is also the author of a novel titled Amanda Wakes Up.
