Are Sam and Peter Still Together?

Sam and Peter are still together as of Season 9 Episode 8 of Bachelor in Paradise. During their double date, the couple engaged in games like “Twerktastic Showdown” and “Blind Besos.”

Sam and Peter’s decision to end their relationship after the date shows that things were going well between them. They are still a couple in the context of Bachelor in Paradise, though it will be interesting to watch how their relationship develops in the next episodes.

Who is Samantha Picco?

The 35-year-old native of Newfoundland, Sam, got her start in reality TV in 2019 when she made an appearance on Big Brother Canada’s seventh season. Sam, who stated that she owns a beauty salon, characterized herself as gregarious, vibrant, and charming in her Q&A for the show.

She claimed to be able to connect with people quickly, but she also acknowledged that she is a terrible liar. She placed fifth at the end of the season. Sam reappeared on television in 2023, this time on season 2 of Bachelor in Paradise Canada. In week one, Sam showed up and explained that this time around she worked as an entrepreneur.

Sam disclosed her dreams of launching a podcast, having her show, and visiting Japan in her season-long bio. She also disclosed that she is afraid of “spiders, tornados, and commitment.”
She and Lisa Mancini both expressed interest in Cole Medders, which led to her becoming entangled in a romantic triangle while in Paradise. As soon as Cole broke up with Sam, Sam decided to leave Paradise. Sam created SAMBRAND, an online store selling cosmetics, following her Big Brother Canada appearance.

In an interview with the Self(ish) Confidence podcast, Sam disclosed that she initially launched a business during her entrepreneurship course in high school. Based on her experience as a self-taught makeup artist with a background in mobile makeup artistry, SAMBRAND was released years later. Sam revealed on the podcast that she had ADHD and that her inability to concentrate on uninteresting things eventually inspired her to start her own business.

Who is Peter Cappio?

Peter Cappio competed in The Bachelorette’s 20th season. He was gone after the first week. For the ninth season of Bachelor in Paradise, he made a comeback. Peter is prepared to locate a copilot. Despite having the ideal job that has allowed him to travel the world, this commercial aviator has yet to find true love.

Peter calls himself “the life of the party” and wishes that his future spouse would have the same upbeat disposition. Peter genuinely thinks that his future wife is out there and regards himself as a man of faith. He is eager to meet the Bachelorette and find out if she is the ideal person he has been searching for.

Never having the opportunity to fly his parents anywhere, he finally got the chance. Peter Cappio Career

Peter Cappio, an airline pilot, has traveled the world as part of his career, something he highlighted on “The Bachelorette.” Known as “the life of the party,” Peter is a devout man who was still looking for his true love even with his ideal career.

Bachelor in Paradise Season 9

On September 28, 2023, the ninth season of Bachelor in Paradise debuted, with Jesse Palmer serving as the host once again. Sayulita, in the Mexican state of Vallarta-Nayarit, served as the location for the filming. Wells Adams made a comeback to the bartending role for the sixth consecutive season.

Due to the 2023 Writers Guild of America strike, the season was originally shifted from its customary Monday and Tuesday timeslot to Thursday after The Golden Bachelor’s premiere. This season, Hannah Brown, Katie Thurston, and Charity Lawson, all former Bachelorettes made cameos.

Brayden Bowers and Katherine Izzo were officially announced as contestants during the Bachelorette season 20 Men Tell All special on August 14, 2023.

During the Bachelorette season 20 After the Final Rose special on August 21, 2023, Aaron Bryant, Brooklyn Willie, and former Bachelorette Rachel Recchia confirmed their participation as contestants.

After Becca Kufrin in season seven, Recchia is the second lead to ever return to Paradise as a contestant. On August 25, 2023, the remaining members of the original cast were revealed.
