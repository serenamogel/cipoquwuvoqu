Breakfast Trail Mix

This easy and delicious Breakfast Trail Mix is great for busy mornings, after school snacks, and game time! Filled with lots of goodness, this trail mix is the perfect combination of crunchy, sweet, and salty!

Course Breakfast Cuisine American Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 4 Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 1 cup pistachios, shelled
  • 1 cup dried banana chips
  • 2 cups Honey Nut Cheerios (or your favorite cereal)
  • 1 1/2 cups yogurt covered pretzels
  • 3/4 cup white chocolate chips (optional)
  • 3/4 cup miniature marshmallows (optional)

Instructions

  • Combine all of the ingredients in a large bowl and toss to ensure ingredients are evenly distributed.

  • Store in an airtight container.

Notes

Tip: I like to portion these out in small snack size containers so my kids (and I!) can just grab and go! Other variations: I've given some suggestions in the post but to recap, other variations might include, roasted almonds, yogurt covered raisins, pretzels sticks, different types of cereal, and if you'd like to sweeten the trail mix, try chocolate chips, M&M's, etc.
