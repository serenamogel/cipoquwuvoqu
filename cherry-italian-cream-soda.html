Cherry Italian Cream Soda

Find out how easy it is to make your own Cherry Italian Cream Sodas at home!

Course Beverage Cuisine American Keyword cherry italian cream soda Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Total Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 4 Calories 67kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 6 oz club soda
  • 2 oz Cherry flavored Torani syrup or your favorite flavor
  • 1 oz heavy cream or half and half
  • ice

Instructions

  • Fill a tall glass two thirds full with ice.

  • Pour in flavored syurp.

  • Top off with club soda.

  • Float the cream on top.

  • Stir just before drinking.

  • If you are experiencing curdling, try mixing the syrup and cream first and then adding the club soda.
