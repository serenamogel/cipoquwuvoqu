Kiyotaka Furushima

Kiyotaka Furushima (Japanese: 古島清孝 Furushima Kiyotaka) is a Japanese voice actor for the Pokémon anime.

Biography

Furushima was born on April 19, 1979, in Tochigi Prefecture, Japan, and became a voice actor as an adult. Furushima began voicing characters in the Pokémon anime toward the midway point of Pokémon the Series: Ruby and Sapphire. Beginning with Pokémon the Series: Diamond and Pearl, he started voicing more notable characters such as Paul. Furushima is currently affiliated with Tokyo Actor's Consumer's Cooperative Society.
