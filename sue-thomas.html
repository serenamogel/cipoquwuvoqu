Biography

Sue Thomas was born in Ohio on May 24, 1950. A famous lip-reader and author, she published Silent Night, a memoir of her experiences as the first deaf undercover investigator for the FBI. She worked for the FBI during the late 1970s and early 1980s. She was portrayed by actress Deanne Bray in the television series, Sue Thomas: F.B.Eye. During her childhood, she was a free-style skating champion. She later received a degree in political science from Springfield College.
