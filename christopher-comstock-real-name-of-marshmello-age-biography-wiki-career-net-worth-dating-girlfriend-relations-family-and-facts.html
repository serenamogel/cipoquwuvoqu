Professional Career Christopher Comstock aka Marsmello

In terms of his profession, Marshmello first earned international attention in early 2015 by uploading remixes online. Joytime, his debut studio album, was released in January 2016, and included the lead track and debut song “Keep It, Mello.”

Monstercat, a Canadian record label, released the platinum-certified track “Alone” in May 2016. It debuted at number 60 on the US Billboard Hot 100 and became his first single to be certified platinum in the US and Canada, with over 1 million approved sales.
